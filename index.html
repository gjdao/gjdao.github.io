Giang "Jenn" Dao

Tech Product Builder and Blockchain Adopter

Hi everyone, my name is Giang 'Jenn' Dao. I was born and raised in Vietnam. I'm a current graduate student in Information System Engineering & Management, with a concentration on Blockchain Technologies.

Prior to grad school, I had 2 years working in consulting services for cloud computing companies in Washington, D.C. Back in 2019, I acquired my Bachelor's Degree in Public Accounting and Finance (Magna Cum Laude) with Statistics minor.

Growing up in Vietnam, a country with history of male dominance, I have never considered tech product as a feasibile career option for me. However, I always love Math and solving problems.

Outside the 9-5 work life, I also find myself passionate about entrepreneurship so I have remained an active role in a startup community. There I could meet and get to know many entrepreneurs with brilliant product ideas. I enjoy talking to them and learning how they bring their products from scratch to reality and create value to many people. In this way, I realized I would love to pursue a career in product development and management. It led me to the decision to go back to graduate school, and focus on becoming a tech/blockchain product manager.

I am also a big fan of role play video games, Netflix’s Black Mirror series, Beeple's artwork, and museums - especially any immersive museum exhibitions like Van Gogh, ARTECHOUSE. So you can see why I am very into the Metaverse. My future goal is to become an entrepreneur, a female tech founder with a vision of the future.

At this point of my career, I am looking foward to building cool products, and currently looking for product management opportunities for Blockchain/Metaverse companies in the US.
